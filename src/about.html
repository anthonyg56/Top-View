About Top View

Topview is a social media platform that encourages entrepreneurial individuals under the age of 30 while also providing an outlet to share ideas, content or their personal brand. The Idea of “TopView” suggests that entrepreneurs who are at the “Top” of their field, have a different “View” at whatever he/she does because they have overcame adversity in their field to become successful. Topview seeks such qualified entrepreneurs and explore their stories, trails and tribulations. By doing so, we hope to influence “young” and “older” entrepreneurs who may seek to be in the same situation someday or set them towards the right direction. Topview will try to be as versatile as possible, talking to entrepreneurs in every field ranging from artists to local innovators in an effort to inspire others to follow their own passion.


If you beleive you are making a similar impact in your local community, feel free to contact us and we will be more than happy to display your journey for others to see.
